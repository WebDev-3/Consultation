About Me

Healing Minds with Empathy & Science

I am Dr. Shafeeq Ahammed, a Consultant Psychiatrist dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care. My approach is rooted in deep listening and personalised treatment — because every person's journey is unique.

What I Treat

Areas of Expertise

Mental health challenges are deeply personal. I provide thoughtful, individualised care for a range of psychiatric conditions.

😔

Depression & Mood Disorders

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of depression, bipolar disorder, and other mood-related conditions.

😰

Anxiety & Panic Disorders

Generalised anxiety, panic attacks, social anxiety, phobias, and OCD — tailored therapy and medication management.

😴

Sleep Disorders

Insomnia, hypersomnia, and sleep disturbances linked to stress, anxiety, or other underlying conditions.

🧩

ADHD & Developmental

Assessment and management of ADHD in adults and children, along with developmental and behavioural challenges.

💊

Addiction Psychiatry

Support and treatment for substance use disorders, de-addiction counselling, and relapse prevention.

🫂

Trauma & PTSD

Healing from past trauma, PTSD, and grief through evidence-based therapeutic approaches and medication.
